Receive Call Inbound voice call example
 
To receive a test inbound call:
 
Log into your VoiceShot account.
Open the inbound campaign named Developer IVR Demo v5.0

On the Campaign Checklist, click Test Campaign

Enter the number you will be calling from.
Call (800) 284-8509 from that number.  This number is for the free trial only.  With a paid account, you get your own phone number.
Watch the Call Events come in during the call.  Look for the key presses "1234"  This example accepts "1234" as valid input and directs the campaign to play the Valid Pin sound file.  This is controlled by line 66 in eventsincall.php  If a sequence other than "1234" is entered, Invalid Pin is played.
Watch the Call Summary come in at the end of the call (if configured).
 
In a production environment, you can use Call Events or Call Summary or both.  Removing a URL from your VoiceShot account will disable that particular function.
