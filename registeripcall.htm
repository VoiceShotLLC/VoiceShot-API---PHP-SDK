Configure Account Outbound voice call example
 
  Register your IP
For this example, you need to enter the IP address of your Web server or application in your VoiceShot account.  To do this:
 
• Log into your VoiceShot account.
• Open the outbound campaign named Developer TTS Demo v5.0.
•

From the menu bar, click Campaign --> Options

• Click the Developer tab.
• Enter the IP address of your Web server or application under Registered IPs and click Save. You can register multiple IP addresses.
 
  Authorize your Caller ID
To initiate a phone call through the API, you must include a Caller ID. Before you include a Caller ID, it must first be authorized. To authorize your Caller IDs:
 
• Log into your VoiceShot account.
•

From the menu bar, click Developer --> Authorize CallerIDs

• There are 3 methods to authorize a Caller ID. More..
 
 
  Configure Call Summary URL*
A Call Summary is a collection of data about a phone call sent to your application at the end of the phone call.  For example, a Call Summary includes information such as whether the call was answered or not, key press information and more.  To receive Call Summaries for this example, do the following:
 
• Log into your VoiceShot account.
• Open the outbound campaign named Developer TTS Demo v5.0.
•

From the menu bar, click Campaign --> Options

• Click the Developer tab.
• For Call Summary URL, enter:
http://yourserver/installdir/summaryoutcall.php
• For more information on Call Summary, click here.
 
In the URL above, replace "yourserver" with the Web address of your Web server and "installdir" with the directory you installed the example in
