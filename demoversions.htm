Version 5.0 Demonstration Campaigns
 
This example code works in conjunction with two demonstration campaigns installed in your VoiceShot account.  They are:
   
• Developer TTS Demo v5.0
  You can see this by clicking Campaign --> Open --> Outbound Phone Calls from the tool bar in your account.
   
• Developer IVR Demo v5.0
  You can see this by clicking Campaign --> Open --> Inbound Phone Calls from the tool bar in your account.
   
• Developer Text Message Demo v5.0
  You can see this by clicking Campaign --> Open --> Text Messaging from the tool bar in your account.
   
If you have older demonstration campaigns in your account that don't include "v5.0" in their names or are missing one of the demonstration campaigns above, do the following to obtain the v5.0 versions:
     
• Delete the older existing demo campaign(s)
  Click Campaign --> Open from the tool bar in your account.
  Select the name of the older demonstration campaign and click Open.
  Click Campaign --> Delete
     
• Re-download a code example
  Click Developer --> Code Examples
  Choose an example language: ASP, .NET, PHP or CFM
     
• Verify you now have the v5.0 demo campaigns
  Click Campaign --> Open --> and verify you see the the appropriate v5.0 demonstration campaign(s).
